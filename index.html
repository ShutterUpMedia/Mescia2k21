About

Mescia 2k21 is one of the biggest vibrant techno management technical meet organized by the department of Computer Technology - PG (M.Sc Software Systems) of Kongu Engineering College, Perundurai. This year the meet commences on 10th April 2021. It is initiated with an aim of providing opportunities to the student community to bring out the immense talent in the competitions modeled to test the technical acumen, programming skills of the participants. The events is experimented with Tech Thoughts, Bug Smashing, Qgramming, Picto Coding and Iconiz which inspired young minds.

So, what are you waiting for?

Tech Thoughts

Thought provides you a platform to unleash your talent by means of presentation and showcase your ideas and just let it flow through your words in front of esteemed panel of Judges.

The team can contain minimum of 1 and maximum of 3 participants.

Bug Smashing

Bug smashing is a solo event to demonstrate your talents in debugging(spoting and removing errors). The 1st round is conducted through google forms and 2nd is through hankerrank.

Qgramming

A Qgramming refers to a short test of knowlegde including multiple choices and they are tasked with masterminding to write a code.

It is a Solo event.

Picto Coding

Picto Coding ia an event to test your coding skills and also understanding ability. It is an coding event only, but quite different from other coding Hackathons. The main process in this event is to understand (or) find the question that are given through pictures. In this event, there are 3 rounds.

The team can contain minimum of 1 and maximum of 2 participants.

Iconiz

Iconiz is the event where you will create the logo for the given constraints with your creativity. A logo is a visual symbol used to instantly identify a company, organization, product, or brand. Logos may show an entity's name spelled out with letters or they may be abstract designs, such as the Nike stripe.

It is a Solo event.
